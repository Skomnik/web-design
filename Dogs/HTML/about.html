About Us:

Welcome to The Kennels! We are a small boarding kennel dedicated to providing your furry family members with a comfortable and luxurious stay.

Our facility boasts 16 individual kennels, ensuring that each dog receives the attention and care they deserve. Our three full-time staff members are committed to making sure your dogs are happy, healthy, and well-exercised while you are away.

Trust us to take care of your pets like they were our own.
